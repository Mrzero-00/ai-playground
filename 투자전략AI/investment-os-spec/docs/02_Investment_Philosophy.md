# Investment Philosophy

## Long-term
Goal:
- Own exceptional businesses for many years.

Evaluation:
- Business Quality
- Valuation
- Opportunity Cost
- Portfolio Fit

## Momentum
Goal:
Capture shorter-term asymmetric opportunities.

Evaluation:
- Relative Strength
- Volume
- Sector Rotation
- Catalyst
- Risk/Reward

Important:
The two strategies NEVER share scoring logic.

Portfolio Engine combines them.
